The Story Behind SalientBones

SalientBones was created long before it was actually intended to even be a Discord bot. Originally, it was created to troll other users by sending messages through the Discord bot, pretending to be a super intelligent AI speaking to the users interacting with it. But, after it stopped working, SalientBones, then simply named "MicrosoftPain •~•" after my own username, was just sitting there on Discord, offline. After a few months, in 2022, I decided to sit down and actually make the Discord bot, well, a Discord bot. Today, it runs on Autocode's infrastructure 24/7, executing all the commands I can dream of and code in.
